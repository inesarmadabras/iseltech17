About

ISELTech is a 3-day conference open to students, workers and technology enthusiasts who want to acquire new knowledge and demonstrate their skills, in a space open to discussion between participants and speakers.

Having happened the last edition in 2013, it returns this year in force and with a new format, full with talks and workshops - and with the usual recruitment and exhibition space, open to everyone. The last day is booked for a discussion panel on Cybersecurity with very special guests. This Tech conference is the result of a partnership between the IEEE ISEL Student Brunch and Google Developers Group Portugal
