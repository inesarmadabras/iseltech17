Another community that support us is 0xOPOSEC - An independent group of IT security enthusiasts which support all kinds of knowledge sharing in various areas, from network, applications, mobile and web, among others.

Founded in January 2016 in Porto, this community organizes meetups which happen thanks to the work of volunteers who help without any kind of reward other than to get more knowledge and some partner companies from the Porto area.

They will be with us on May 27, with André Baptista (CrackMe Up - An Introduction to Binary Reverse Engineering) and José Sousa (WORKSHOP Web Exploitation 101).

We want to send a big thank you to Oposec for the offered support and can't wait to meet you at ISELTech together with them!
