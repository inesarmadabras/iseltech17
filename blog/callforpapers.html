Who should speak at the ISELTech'17?

  • Developers
  • Software Companies (apps, tools, frameworks etc.)
  • Universities or Research Institutions
  • Individuals with something to share!

 The deadline for submitting proposals is March 30th. We will notify applicants of accepted proposals by April 15th, 2017.

What is it about?

We are looking for tech talks and hands-on sessions on the following areas:

  • Mobile
  • Web
  • Design
  • Cloud & BigData
  • Gaming & VR/AR
  • IoT
  • UX/UI
  • Hardware
  • Industry 4.0 & Higher Voltage Technologies
  • Cybersecurity

However, if you've got a great talk and want to share it with us, then please submit it!

You can submit as many talks as you want (in English or in Portuguese)!

What do I get out of it?

The right question is What will I learn? It's an unique opportunity to network, expand your knowledge and find solutions to problems, to present your ideas, for people to meet you and here's the best part: to Have Fun!

I already submitted my talk. I'll book the trip now!

Please, wait. There is no guarantee that your submission will be put onto the conference agenda! The program committee will deliberate and select proposals that are expected to generate the most interest. All applicants will be informed of the status of their proposal by 15 April, 2017.


Will I be published in an IEEE magazine?

No. IEEE Student Branch ISEL only belongs to the organization, such as Google Developers Group Lisbon. The form is just for participation as a speaker at the ISELTech'17 conference.
