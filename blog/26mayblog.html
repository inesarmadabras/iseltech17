26 May

26th of May 2017, second day of ISEL Tech! Arcade machines and a PlayStation VR, already sounds good!

First session of this day is about Low-code Mobile Apps, with Nuno Mauricio & Cesar Afonso. “What I've learned as an unexperienced developer building a worldwide used app” will be presented by Fábio Carballo and we will continue with “A ERA das Single Page Applications” by Mónica Rodrigues from Geek Girls Portugal.

“Making it possible”, by Rogério Rocha from Impossible is about how technology can improve people’s lives and still be sustainable. “Erlang is awesome!” says Alexandre Rodrigues which will share with us this functional programming language created 30 years ago at Ericsson that powers the Billions of messages sent through WhatsApp every day.

Later in the afternoon Francisco Franco - Android apps & Kernel developer, will speak about “What is to be an independent Android developer in Portugal an in the world”, challenges and an independent path into a competitive technology world.

Our friends from ITPeople will have a demo with a Product Demonstration of Microsoft Hololens.

Rui Alves, from Rupeal and KWAN will predict some of “O que nos espera no futuro?” regarding opening an own company and what´s waiting for us on the work market. And if we’re talking about work and getting out of the school benches, Ricardo Fiel will point to “O que não nos ensinam na escola”, a very valuable session for first year students and not only.

Attendees are invited to explore the current state of the VR industry, its current challenges and how to get into VR meanwhile session of Daniela Fontes from Mimicry Games. This demo has been built for HTC Vive.

The Who´s Who of Google Cloud Platform, by Randy Gupta is a session offered by GDG Düsseldorf. Panayotis Tzinis is the last speaker of the day - we'll finish big with "Getting started with Machine Learning"

The Sampling Line Lda. will be at ISEL Tech with Think3D, PTServidor and RetroShop.pt Throughout the event will be available demonstrations of 3D Printing, Arcade machines and retro-gaming consoles to play! Stop by the exhibitor, we guarantee magic moments! You can experiment all this on 26th of May, during all day. Come join us!
