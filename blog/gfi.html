GFI is a SILVER Sponsor of ISELTech'17!

Gfi Group is one of the most high-performing digital and IT service companies on the market. Since 2011, Gfi Group has experienced an exceptional growth, having now more than 14,500 employees and a strong presence in 16 countries – France, Spain, Portugal, Belgium, Morocco, Switzerland, Luxembourg, Ivory Coast, Poland, England, Romania, Angola, USA, Mexico, Colombia, and Brazil.

In Portugal, Gfi has been present for over 20 years, conquering the domestic market by positioning itself as an important and reliable technological partner of its customers.

With a team of over 600 highly qualified professionals, Gfi is positioned as one of the leading companies in this sector, supporting its customers at all stages of a technological transformation project, from the initial stage of consulting until the implementation, control and maintenance of the Information Systems (IS).

Gfi offers a broad set of skills to a wide range of services and solutions tailored to market needs in the fields of Banking & Insurance, Public Administration, Health, Telecommunications & Media, Industry & Utilities, and Transport & Distribution.

Gfi acts on five key business areas: Consulting, Application Services, Infrastructure Services, Enterprise Solutions and Software. This allows a comprehensive and cross-monitoring of the different areas of its customers' Information Systems, supporting them from the earliest stages of planning, implementation and subsequent maintenance.
