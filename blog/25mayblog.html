25 May

ISEL Tech has been thought of as 3 days event with the aim to approach some interesting fields in technology as Cloud, Web, Gaming, Cibersecurity, Android.

On 25th of May the focus will be on Cloud, 5G, Angular, Nativescript.
The day will start with a talk of @Rodrigo Antunes, Premier Field Engineer @Microsoft Portugal, which will show us in his session how looks the success of Azure Web Applications.

After this, @Pedro Azevedo from @Findmore Consulting will demonstrate how to leverage the integration between Microsoft Office 265 and Microsoft Dynamic CRM for a more agile context and organization.

Right after lunch, @Luis Gonçalves, software engineer @Bold International will ofer some... cake! Curious about which cake? C# make! Tool for automatization of builds and C# of which principles and functionalities will be explored together with Luis, followed by a script progressively built for a typical .NET scenario.

The day will continue with @Pedro Couto from Accenture, then with @Sebastian Witalec about mobile applications, Nativescript and Javascript. Sebastian is from the Core Team of NativeScript!

In the afternoon our IEEE guest is Rui Luís Aguiar, an IEEE ComSoc Distingueshed Lecturer which will talk us about some research developed for 5G networks.

Exactly after the coffee break we'll have the opportunity to build a roboarmy with Angular, an application from zero to a functional stage, following to talk with @Miguel Ribeiro from @GFI Portugal about costs in the era of digital transformation and to end the day with Celso Martinho - founder of Sapo and BrightPixel. Celso was responsible for the largest wave of innovation in the country and will tell us about his experience.

This is the schedule of the first day at ISEL Tech. Sounds good? Come with us!
