Impossible is a GOLD Sponsor of ISELTech'17!

Impossible is a global digital product design and innovation company. We use technology, art and design to solve some of the world's seemingly impossible problems.

We bring measurable innovation to global clients such as Samsung, Google, Intel, Roche, as well as impactful startups such as Azimo, Braveno, Tabl, and Babylon Health. We also experiment and build our own products, so we can innovate, learn, and share the rewards with everyone. We’re currently working on a productivity Slack bot, high tech bracelets for couples separated by distance, and AR-powered app that will help people to flesh out their creative selves.

We function as a network that spans across many countries and can deploy 'pods'​ of experts to anywhere in the world for our clients and partners. We have studios in London, Lisbon, San Francisco, New York and Brisbane. But ultimately we’re all over the place - we quickly dislodge teams to wherever the new challenge is calling us, and sometimes migrate to work from inspiring locations to get our creative juices flowing.

We know that to create impactful and purposeful products and services, we need to have a healthy, engaging and diverse work environment. That’s why we try to shape work around our lives, not the other way around.

Impossible is a certified B Corporation, which demonstrates our dedication to running a socially and environmentally sustainable business. .
