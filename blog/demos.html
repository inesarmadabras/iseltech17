Mimicry Games - HTC Vive

Mimicry Games will demonstrate a playable prototype of a project whose title is not yet officially announced. This demo was designed for the HTC Vive.

HTC VIVE enables immersive experiences in virtual reality through the Room Scale system, which allows mapping an area of play in which it is possible to move with great freedom. In addition to the Room Scale, Vive offers a high precision hand tracking solution (hand mapping).

The Sampling Line - Arcade Games

The Sampling Line, Lda. will be at the #iseltech with Think3D, PTServidor and RetroShop.pt Throughout the event will be available demonstrations of 3D Printing, Arcade machines and retrogaming consoles to play! Stop by the exhibitor, we guarantee magic moments!

The Lisbon VR - HTC Vive (Multiplayer)

The Lisbon VR will be the first room-scale virtual reality arcade in Lisbon. We believe that RV is one of the most spectacular experiences today and, like everything else in life, it is best if it is shared.

Join your friends and come kill zombies in #iseltech, escape the robots assassins, solve the most challenging escape rooms and more! Follow the project on facebook or at thelisbonvr.com!

ITPeople- Microsoft Hololens

You'll get a chance to try the Hololens on May 25-26 and you'll still be able to watch a 15-minute product demo from It People Innovation - a pioneer company in the Augmented Reality market in Portugal and the first Portuguese company to publish an app for These revolutionary glasses.

This is an opportunity to get to know they work and experience firsthand the Augmented Reality solutions they have already developed!
